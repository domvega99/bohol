About Judd's Empanada

            "WE BELIEVE
             IN BRINGING
PEOPLE TOGETHER
   AROUND
     GREAT FOOD."

It’s started in June 2014 located in Maria Clara Street. CPG Avenue fronting Tamblot road. There empanada was passed to generations to generations, until came the legacy of mommy Alma that improves there empanada business. There empanada is a stuffed bread or pastry baked or fried. They made there empanada by folding a dough or bread patty around the stuffing. The stuffing can consist of a variety of meats, vegetables, or even fruits. There empanada is famous for its delicate and unique taste that’s why people always buy there empanada.
